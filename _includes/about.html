About Us

Quality Asheville Desserts

Lilly May's is committed to offering large and small format hand crafted desserts made from locally sourced and sustainable ingredients by passionate human beings. Lilly May's is here to make your special occasion that much more special with a little something sweet. Whether you are having a formal office brunch and need coffee service and pastries, a lavish wedding with a large dessert table, or a baby shower brunch with yogurt jars and cupcakes, we are here to provide you with the highest quality sweets and the highest quality service.

Locacted in Asheville, North Carolina, Lilly May's is created by Heather Gressett, one of Asheville's top pastry chefs.
